Ethnic Link Services

View their website here

Ethnic Link Services provide assistance to older people from non-English speakingbackgrounds to remain living independently at home.
Bilingual and bicultural staff provide interpreting services, liaise between providers, help with correspondence, get people involved in activities and run language-specific programs. They also provide home visits and social support for vulnerable or isolated people from culturally and linguistically diverse backgrounds.
