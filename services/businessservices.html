Business Services

Private services for potential and new migrants

Important Information!

You should only use Registered Migrations Agents to help with your migration journey.

You can search for Agents, or check whether your Migration Agent is registered with the 'Migration Agents Registration Authority' here.

Work Visa Lawyer

View their website here

Work Visa Lawyers provide legal advice and guidance for people looking to migrate, including choosing the correct visa and ensuring you meet all the requirements.

Migration Solutions

View their website here

Migration Solutions is a team of Registered Migration agents and support staff that can assist you with your migration application.

Welcome2Adelaide

View their website here

Welcome2Adelaide offers a range of tailored service packages to introduce you to Adelaide, and help you settle in when you arrive.

PAX Migration Australia

View their website here

PAX Migration are migration agents who can help you prepare your visa applications, for all visas except protection visas.
