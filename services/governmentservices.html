Government Services

Publiclyfunded services available for migrants

Office of the Migration Agents Registration Authority

View their website here

The Office of the Migration Agents Registration Authority (MARA) provide a range of information on migration agents, including a search function to find Registered Migration Agents.

Immigration SA

View their website here

Immigration SA offers a range of services, including information on various Visa types, support services, information sessions and information about living and working in South Australia.

Department of Human Services

View their website here

The Department of Human Services provides many support services for migrants and refugees, including financial support and welfare, Medicare, and other support services. They also offer a multilingual phone service if you need to speak to someone in your preferred language.

Immigration and Citizenship - Department of Home Affairs

View their website here

The Immigration and Citizenship section of the Department of Home Affairs also provides a range of important services for potential immigrants, including applying for visas, exploring visa options and citizenship information.

Legal Services Commission

View their website here

The Legal Services Commission offers a free legal advisory service to immigrants and refugees through its Adelaide office. They offer a range of advisory services on topics such as onshore protection visas, visa cancellations and appeals, and citizenship.
