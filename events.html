Oz Asia Festival

17 October 2019 - 03 November 2019

Get more information about this event here

OzAsia Festival is Australia’s leading contemporary arts festival engaging with Asia. It presents a bold and exciting line-up of boundary-pushing contemporary arts each year and is revered by audiences and critics alike.

Loy Krathong Festival - Thai Festival of Light

09 November 2019

Get more information about this event here

Loy Krathong, also known as Thai Festival of Lights is one of Thailand's most enchanting festival. Loy Krathong takes place on the evening of the full moon of the 12th month in the traditional Thai lunar calendar. This marks the date of the festival change every year. Usually it falls in the month of November. This year we celebrate Loy Krathong Festival on Saturday, 9 November 2019.
Experience the beautiful traditions of Thai culture at Bonython Park Loy Krathong Festival. Featuring enchanting lighting treatments, traditional games, and a range of delicious Thai food offerings.

English conversation meet-up

04 October 2019 - 31 December 2019

Get more information about this event here

Join volunteer Thor from the Adelaide English as a Second Language Group, and others to practice holding longer conversations. This group is aims to encourage English as a Second Language (ESL) people to practice their English in a friendly atmosphere, to learn about others' languages and cultures, and to make new friends.
Sessions are held fortnightly on Fridays.

Glendi Greek Festival 2019

29 November 2019 - 01 December 2019

Get more information about this event here

Glendi is an annual festival that celebrates all things Hellenic.
Glendi’s origins date back to 1978. It was originally established as a fundraising event between the Lions Club of Adelaide Hellenic and West Torrens Football Club. It has grown in its history, developing into an acclaimed multicultural festival. It is arguably one of the original ‘food and wine’ festivals in South Australia.
