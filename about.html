About

South Australian Futures is a semester-long theme of The Enquiring Mind.

Dornwell

There are a range of challenges that emerge from the settlement process: language barriers hinder social interaction, education and the employment process; cultural and religious differences facilitate segregation and prevent integration, and available settlement services, while improving, can always be improved. The goal of this project is to investigate and alleviate the hardships associated with settlement by directing new and potential migrants to support services, educational services, event calendars and social forums.

We hope to build a foundation through which migrants and Australian-born citizens alike can celebrate cultural differences, by bringing people together through music, food and other meaningful social connections. In this regard, we hope to live up to our country’s ‘proudly multicultural’ reputation, and continue to keep Adelaide as one of the ten most liveable cities in the world.

The overarching goal of this project is to make Adelaide a city with a global reputation for being multicultural, welcoming and easy to move to. We hope to attract bright young minds from all over the world to bring their talents to Adelaide and secure the cultural and economic future of the state.
