Visa Rules

What you will need to legally migrate to Adelaide

Important Information

This advice is not legal advice! For detailed advice, tailored to your situation, please contact a Registered Migration Agent who can help guide you through this process.

You can find Registered Migration Agents from the Migration Agents Registration Authority here

Visa Options

Australia offers a range of visa options, each with specific requirements and restrictions. It is very important that you have the correct visa for your situation. Most of these visas have an application fee, and many require specific documents to be submitted.

  • Visitor Visas - short term visas for people visiting Australia
  • Studying and Training Visas - for people looking to study or learn new skills
  • Family and Partner Visas - for people migrating to join a family member already in Australia
  • Working and Skilled Visas - for people with skills or capital to start a business in Australia
  • Refugee and Humanitarian Visas - for refugees and people seeking safety
  • Other Visas - specific niche visas that will not apply to most migrants

For a full list of visa types, click here

Applying for a Visa

You can use the Department of Home Affairs website to apply for a visa. Follow the prompts to find out which visas are suitable for your situation.
