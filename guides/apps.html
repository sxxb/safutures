Essential Apps for SA Life

Apps

Many Australian banks, phone service providers and even government institutions offer apps to help you acces their services.

Express Plus Centrelink

Click here for more information

Express Plus Centrelink is the Department of Human Services' app for handling your welfare payments, appointments and information. You can track your claims, view your payment history and update your personal details from the palm of your hand.

MetroMate

Click here for more information

MetroMate is Adelaide Metro's app, designed to help you use our public transit system. You can plan trips, find your nearest stop, set alarms for upcomming journeys and more.

Park Adelaide

Click here for more information

Park Adelaide is an app provided by the Adelaide City Council, to help you use Adelaide's paid parking system. Top up your parking online, receive alerts when your parking in about to expire and view available parking spaces.

Gumtree

Click here for more information

Gumtree is Australia's marketplace - with Gumtree you can buy and sell from other Australians, search for jobs and more. Gumtree is available from their website and also as a downloadable app.

Seek

Click here for more information

Seek is a job listing website and app. Businesses from all over Australia list their available jobs on Seek, and job seekers can upload their resume and apply for jobs at the click of a button. Seek is available as a website and as an app.
