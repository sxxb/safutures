Paying for Parking

How to use Adelaide City Council's paid parking

Parking in Adelaide

For more information about parking in Adelaide, visit the City of Adelaide website here

Paying with Coins and Cash

Purchasing a parking ticket with coins (or cash on selected machines) is quick and simple.

  1. Insert coins for the duration required (refer to ticket machine screen).
  2. Press the TICKET button to accept and print ticket (or press CANCEL to cancel the transaction and be refunded the monies inserted).
  3. Collect the printed ticket and display it clearly on the passenger side dashboard of your vehicle with the expiry date and time facing up.

Paying with Credit Card

Visa and MasterCard are accepted where ticket machines accept credit card payments. A transaction fee may apply to credit card payments. While different machines may have slightly different processes, the directions below will help you pay by credit card on most machines.

  1. Insert credit card as shown on the machine. Press the Up and/DOWN button to select the duration required and fee desired (refer to ticket machine screen).
  2. View the machine display for the status of the transaction. Once approved the machine screen will display when to remove your credit card.
  3. The machine will print the parking ticket and dispense it into the collection tray (typically within 30 seconds).
  4. Collect the printed ticket and display it clearly on the passenger side dashboard of your vehicle with the expiry date and time facing up.
