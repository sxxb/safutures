Where to Live

How to find a place to live that suits your lifestyle

Choosing where to live.

Finding a place to live in Adelaide can be tough, as there are so many places to choose from! If you're not sure where in Adelaide to live, this guide is for you.

Real Estate Websites

Australia has a few real estate websites, RealEstate.com.au,Homeley and Domain are just a few examples. These sites are very useful for searching for places to buy or rent. You can search by location, filter by property type and price range, and look at sold properties to help understand the Adelaide property market.

Western & Beachside Suburbs

Adelaide is lucky enough to have a wide range of beaches all along our western coastline. The western suburbs offer a great combination of beach lifestyle without being far from the Central Business District, and there are suburbs to fit every budget.

Inner South & East

The Inner South and Eastern parts of Adelaide are the premium areas, offering lush treelined streets, gorgeous heritage homes and happening cafe and shopping precincts. Such a beautiful area comes at a cost, and these parts of Adelaide are a bit more expensive - though there are still bargains to be found!

Northern Suburbs

With new developments happening every year, the northern suburbs are rapidly growing. Perfect for young couples and families, this area is very affordable and is the perfect place to jump into the property market.

Adelaide Hills

Prefer a more rural setting? The Adelaide Hills are a verdant and relaxing escape from the city with a range of historic townships throughout the hills. Travelling into the city is quick and easy via the South Eastern Freeway, and you can be in the Central Businss District in 40 minutes from historic Hahndorf, picturesque Mount Barker or leafy Aldgate.
